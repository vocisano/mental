Concept Mapping

Mental is a concept mapping app for the iPad.

Concept maps organize knowledge in the form of a graph of concepts.

Concepts are represented as a noun phrase enclosed in a rectangular box.

They are connected to other concept boxes by links labeled with a verb or conjunction. These links represent relationships between the connected concepts.

Concept boxes may have relationships to and from several other concept boxes, creating a graph of concepts, or concept map.
