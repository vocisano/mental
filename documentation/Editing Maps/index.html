Editing Maps

How do I add a concept box?

Tap the + button in the toolbar. This will insert a box in the middle of the visible part of the map, and open it in edition mode.

To add a box anywhere on a map, tap that location, and select Add Concept from the concept menu.

How do I move one or more boxes?

Depending on the app settings, you may have to select them first to move them.

If Movement Without Selection is left unchecked in the App Settings, you will have to select them first, by tapping them. Then simply drag them around.

If Movement Without Selection is checked in the App Settings, you can simply drag a box around, without prior selection.

The Movement Without Selection option adds fluidity when beginning a new map. When the map gets bigger, and you need more control, it's better to uncheck that option.

How do I enlarge a map?

Simply drag boxes around to enlarge the map. This will only work if you have more than one box, though.

How do I edit the text in a box or label?

Tap the box to select it. Tap Edit in its context menu. Alternatively, perform a double-tap on the box.

How do I connect boxes?

Tap the origin box to select it. Tap Connect in its context menu; the box blinks red indicating it's in connection mode. Tap the destination box to create the connection.

To add a connector without a label, perform a double-tap on the destination box, instead of a single-tap.

How do I select one or more boxes?

To select a single box, simply tap that box. A green ring appears, indicating the selected state.

To select multiple boxes, tap to select/deselect them. You will then be able to move or delete them in unison.

To deselect all currently selected boxes, tap an empty area of the map.

How do I add a hyperlink to a concept box?

Tap the box to select it. Tap Edit in its context menu. Alternatively, perform a double-tap on the box.

Type or paste in a valid hyperlink in the input accessory view. You can check the link by tapping the Open button in the input accessory view.

How do I navigate to the hyperlink of a concept box?

Tap the box to select it. Tap Open link in its context menu. Alternatively, perform a triple-tap on the box.

How does snapping work?

Snapping will help you align boxes horizontally and vertically. In addition, it lets you align connection labels with the parent and child concepts.
